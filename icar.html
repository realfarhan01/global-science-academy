ICAR

The ICAR exam is essential for students aiming to pursue undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in agricultural universities. Global Science Academy’s ICAR coaching program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in this exam.

Key Features:

  • Expert Guidance: Learn from experienced faculty members specializing in agricultural sciences.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Detailed coaching on all relevant subjects including Agriculture, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Regular Evaluations: Periodic tests to assess knowledge and readiness for the exam.
  • Conceptual Depth: Focus on building a deep understanding of core concepts.
  • Supportive Learning Environment: Interactive classes, doubt-clearing sessions, and personalized mentoring.

Enrollment:

Take the first step towards a rewarding career in agriculture by joining our ICAR coaching program. Enroll now and ensure your success.
